## Employee Management System Database

A MySQL-based system to manage employees, departments, projects, attendance, performance, and benefits.

Features:

- Store employee personal, job, and salary details.

- Manage departments and assign managers.

- Track projects and employee project assignments.

- Record attendance and performance reviews.

- Maintain employee benefits with start/end dates.

- Views for combined employee info and department-wise counts.

- Stored procedures to insert, update, and delete employees efficiently.
